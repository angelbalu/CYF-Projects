Top Things To DO in Bali:

Natural Wonders:

  • Rice Terraces:

    • These terraced rice paddies, located near Ubud, are a must-see for their breathtaking views and photogenic landscapes.

  • Ubud Monkey Forest:

    • Explore a tranquil forest home to a playful monkey population, a unique experience for nature lovers.

  • Uluwatu Temple:

    • Witness a stunning cliffside temple, known for its dramatic sunset views and the Kecak fire dance.

  • Dua:

    • Enjoy beautiful beaches, resorts, and various water sports activities.

  • Gili Islands:

    • Escape to these islands with palm-lined white sand beaches and turquoise waters, perfect for relaxation.

Cultural and Historical Sites:

  • Tanah Lot Temple:
    •

    Visit this iconic temple perched on a rocky outcrop at the edge of the ocean, especially beautiful at sunset.

  • Ubud Palace:
    •

    Explore the traditional Balinese architecture and learn about the region's cultural heritage.
